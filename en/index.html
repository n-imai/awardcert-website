Frequently Asked Questions

Is Award Certificate Creator free to use?

Yes, all basic features are completely free. Choose from a variety of templates and create beautiful award certificates.

Does it work offline?

Creating, saving, and sharing certificates works offline. However, displaying ads requires an internet connection.

What occasions is it suitable for?

Perfect for school events, company awards, club activities, family appreciation, event souvenirs, and more.

What types of templates are available?

We offer 6 designs: gold, rainbow, floral certificates, sakura graduation, birthday cards, and valentine. Choose one that fits your occasion.

Is it suitable for kids awards and rewards?

Absolutely! The app is perfect for kids. Use the rainbow certificate or birthday card designs to reward children for achievements, good behavior, or special occasions.

Can I print the certificates?

Yes! Export certificates as high-quality PNG images and print them at home or at a print shop. The resolution is optimized for professional printing.
