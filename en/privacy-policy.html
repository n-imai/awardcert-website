Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jul 5, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the app handles personal data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected

  • Usage data (Google Analytics)
  • Crash reports
  • Anonymous data for ads

2.2 User-Provided

  • Texts you input in certificates
  • Generated certificate images

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Collected information is used for:

  • Providing app functionality
  • Improving and optimizing the app
  • Resolving technical issues
  • Delivering relevant advertisements

4. Data Storage and Management

4.1 Local Storage

  • All certificate data is stored locally on your device
  • Developers do not have access to your certificate data

4.2 Data Deletion

  • Uninstalling the app deletes all local data
  • You can also delete data from within the app settings

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 This app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • For advertisement delivery
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Analytics for Firebase

  • For app usage analysis
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Analytics 4

  • For website usage analysis
  • Collects only anonymized information
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Advertising

6.1 Types of Ads

This app displays the following types of advertisements:

  • Banner ads (displayed at the bottom of the screen)
  • Interstitial ads (full-screen ads displayed at specific moments, such as after creating a certificate)

6.2 Ad Frequency Limits

  • Interstitial ads are shown with at least 60 seconds between each display
  • Limited to a maximum of 3 times per session

6.3 Ad Delivery

  • This app displays advertisements using Google AdMob
  • Ads are delivered based on non-personally identifiable information
  • You can disable personalized advertising

7. Tracking and Consent

7.1 App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

This app may request tracking permission for ad personalization on devices running iOS 14.5 or later.

  • You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings app
  • Declining permission does not restrict the app's core functionality

7.2 GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) will see a GDPR consent form on first launch.

  • Your consent choice affects ad personalization
  • You can withdraw or change your consent at any time in the app settings

8. Children's Privacy

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect user information.

10. Privacy Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated without prior notice. We will notify users within the app if significant changes are made.

11. Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us via our Contact page.
